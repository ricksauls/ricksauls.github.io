AI + Automation Implementation

Solving Commerce Complexity
with AI + Automation.

Commerce organizations are managing more channels, more data, and more operational complexity than ever before. Walmart.com, Amazon, retail media, social commerce, content management, reporting, competitive monitoring, deductions, and analytics all require time, resources, and specialized expertise.

The Problem
The cost of managing this complexity is often increasing faster than the revenue it generates.

AI + Automation provide an opportunity to create operational leverage without continually adding resources.
The Challenge

Every new commerce channel creates additional work.

  • More data.
  • More reporting.
  • More content management.
  • More competitive monitoring.
  • More operational processes.

Many organizations respond by adding people, agencies, software, and spreadsheets. While often necessary, this approach becomes increasingly difficult and expensive to scale.

The result is that teams spend valuable time collecting information, creating reports, and managing exceptions instead of focusing on activities that drive growth.

The Opportunity

AI + Automation create operational leverage.

By automating repetitive work, surfacing actionable insights, and streamlining operational processes, organizations can improve efficiency without sacrificing visibility or control.

The most effective solutions are rarely generic tools. They are designed around the specific workflows, systems, and challenges of the business.

The examples below demonstrate how AI + Automation can be applied to solve real commerce challenges.

Case Study 02 — Walmart.com
Walmart.com Competitive Intelligence

An AI-powered competitive intelligence platform that continuously tracks Walmart.com performance against competing brands and products. Users configure custom tracking groups, competitor brands, products, and keyword sets, while the platform automatically collects marketplace data, benchmarks performance, monitors content changes, and surfaces competitive trends through an interactive web application.

What traditionally requires teams to manually monitor search rankings, compare product pages, audit competitor content, and track marketplace changes is transformed into a centralized intelligence platform. Stakeholders gain daily visibility into search performance, share of search, pricing, reviews, ratings, content updates, and new competitor activity — all without relying on spreadsheets or manual audits.

  • Configure custom competitor tracking groups by category, product line, or market segment
  • Monitor keyword rankings and share of search across brands and products
  • Track pricing, ratings, reviews, and product performance trends over time
  • Detect competitor content updates, enhanced content changes, and digital shelf activity
  • Centralized web application with dashboards, benchmarking, and historical trend analysis
